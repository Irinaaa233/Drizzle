About Drizzle

All the Fun of Classic Cream Liqueur. Minus the Dairy and Less Sugar.

Drizzle was created because there were no truly indulgent, dairy-free alternatives in the cream liqueur space and we knew there had to be a better option. Drizzle is a female-founded, dairy-free and low-sugar cream liqueur meticulously crafted for both the beverage connoisseur and the casual drinker.

Offered in your choice of oat, pistachio, or coconut milk alternatives, our signature Hazelnut and Vanilla flavors are rich, creamy, and perfect for sipping, mixing, or drizzling over your favorite treats.
